MeetGram Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-03-17

MeetGram is a social communication platform. To provide core features such as messaging, media sharing, and friend discovery, we need to collect and process specific information, including your device's network state, precise and approximate location, contact list, microphone audio, and media files. We also integrate Huawei SDKs to deliver push notifications and monitor app stability.

1. How we collect and use your personal information

We provide you with the following features: Social networking, messaging, and media sharing. To fulfill the requirements of a contract between you and us, we process necessary information. If you do not provide the related information, your use of the features in this app will be affected.

To implement the app features, after obtaining your explicit consent, we need to collect and process your network connection information, precise and approximate location data, contact list details, microphone input (for voice messages), and media gallery access (to send photos and videos).

2. Required permissions

  • Internet: Required to connect to the MeetGram servers to send and receive messages and media.
  • Vibrate: Used to provide haptic feedback for incoming messages and notifications.
  • Location: Requested to allow you to share your location with contacts or find nearby users within the app.
  • Read Contacts: Required to scan your device's phonebook to help you find and connect with friends who are already using MeetGram.
  • Microphone: Needed to allow you to record and send voice messages to your contacts.
  • Read Media & Media Location: Necessary to allow you to select, upload, and share photos and videos from your gallery within your chats.

3. Third-party SDKs

Huawei Software Technologies Co., Ltd. - Push Kit SDK
Type of information collected: Push token, AAID (Anonymous Application ID), and basic device information.
Purpose: To establish a connection with the Huawei push server and deliver real-time push notifications for new messages and alerts.
Usage scenario: When the application receives an incoming message or notification in the background.
Privacy Policy: https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/en/privacy/

4. Managing your personal information

Accessing your information: Settings > Profile (profile details and uploaded media)

Deleting your information / Account: Settings > Account > Delete Account

Withdrawing your consent: HarmonyOS Settings > Apps > MeetGram > Permissions

5. Information storage location and retention period

Retention period: 90 days after account deletion request.

Server location: Europe (or the respective regional servers hosting the MeetGram platform, in compliance with applicable data protection laws).
