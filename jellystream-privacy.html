Jelly Stream Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-21

Jelly Stream is an app provided by Matheus Rodrigues do Vale (hereinafter referred to as "we", "us", or "our") to allow users to stream media from their personal self-hosted servers. This Privacy Statement describes how we process your personal information.

We take your personal information and privacy seriously, and will take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information in accordance with relevant laws and established industry security standards. Jelly Stream acts as a client to connect to your personal media server. We do not collect your media content or credentials. We use Huawei SDKs to improve app stability and display ads.

1. How we collect and use your personal information

We provide you with the following features: Media Streaming. When you use our service, we will need to process some necessary information, so as to enter into a contract. If you do not provide the related information, your use of the features in this app will be affected.

To implement app features, after obtaining your consent, we need to collect your Network connection information and server login credentials.

2. Required permissions

  • Internet (ohos.permission.INTERNET): Required to connect to your personal server and stream media.
  • App Tracking Consent (ohos.permission.APP_TRACKING_CONSENT): Requested to read the open anonymous device ID (OAID) to provide personalized ads via Petal Ads.

3. Third-party SDKs

To provide you with certain features or services, we have integrated software development kits (SDKs) of our partners:

Huawei Software Technologies Co., Ltd. - Petal Ads SDK (Ads Kit)
Type of information collected: OAID, Device identifiers, and network information.
Purpose: To provide and display native advertisements inside the application.
Usage scenario: When the user is browsing the media library.
Privacy Policy: https://consumer.huawei.com/en/privacy/privacy-policy/

Huawei Software Technologies Co., Ltd. - AppGallery Connect SDK (Crash and Analytics)
Type of information collected: App crash logs, device model, and operating system version.
Purpose: To monitor app stability, analyze crashes, and improve application performance.
Usage scenario: When the application is running or encounters a crash.
Privacy Policy: https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/en/privacy/

4. Managing your personal information

Withdrawing your consent: If you want to cancel the permission for personalized ads to process your information, you can go to HarmonyOS Settings > Privacy > Ads and Personalization to withdraw your consent.

5. Information storage location and retention period

We promise that, unless otherwise stipulated by laws or regulations, the retention period for your personal information will be the shortest period necessary to achieve the processing purpose.

The above information will be sent to and stored on the server located in the user's device and the user's personal self-hosted server.

6. Personal Server Disclaimer

Jelly Stream does not host, provide, distribute, or store any media content. Users must provide their own server infrastructure and are solely responsible for the media they stream through the application.
